## Interpreting Results

- **Liquidity > $20K**: decent, lower risk of rug
- **Liquidity $5-20K**: thin, higher slippage and risk
- **Liquidity < $5K**: avoid (default filter)
- **Buy/Sell ratio**: >80% buys = FOMO, be cautious
- **Age < 1h**: very early, highest risk
- **Age 1-4h**: discovery phase, good entry if contract is clean
- **Age 4-24h**: established trend, verify before entry
- **Boosts**: paid promotion, not organic — verify independently
- **RPC 429 errors**: common on public Solana nodes. Always follow up with `onchain-analyzer safety` first for quick contract check before full `analyze`. Avoid `--check-contract` in `scan` — use `safety` for bulk screening. Never trust boost rank alone — always verify contract safety and liquidity before proceeding. Top boosters often have low liquidity (<$10K) and are high-risk pump-and-dumps. ✅ **NEW BEST PRACTICE**: After trending scan, always run `onchain-analyzer safety` + `liquidity` on top 3. Only consider tokens with safety == 45 AND liquidity > $10K for further analysis. This filters out 90% of fake pumps. ⚠️ **CRITICAL**: Even if a token passes safety and liquidity checks, never open a new position if you already hold it. Risk-manager enforces this rule — duplicate positions are blocked. Do not chase multiple entries on the same token, even if it's pumping. ⚠️ **ADDITIONAL**: If `CANNOT_READ_HOLDERS` appears during `analyze`, do not reject the token — it often means >10K holders or proxy contract. Proceed if safety == 45 and liquidity > $10K. This is common on successful memecoins.

## Contract Check Results (--check-contract)

- ✅ `mint: revoked` — safe, no new tokens can be minted
- ❌ `mint: ACTIVE` — dev can print tokens, HIGH RISK
- ✅ `freeze: revoked` — safe, your tokens can't be frozen
- ❌ `freeze: ACTIVE` — dev can freeze your wallet, HIGH RISK

---

## Pitfalls

- **DEXScreener rate limits**: 300 req/min for pair data, 60 req/min for profiles/boosts.
- **Token profiles are self-submitted**: having a profile doesn't mean legitimacy.
- **Contract check uses Solana RPC**: public RPC has rate limits. Use private RPC for production.
- **Not all scams are detectable**: honeypots, hidden mint via proxy, etc. Always DYOR.
- **pairCreatedAt** is in milliseconds since epoch.
